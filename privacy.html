Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

We value your privacy and recognize the sensitivity of personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how VinylScan App ("VinylScan", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal data when you use the VinylScan mobile app, website, and related services (collectively, the "VinylScan Service").

This Privacy Policy should be read together with our Terms of Use at https://vinylscanapp.com/terms.html.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data: email address, login credentials, account identifiers, subscription status.
  • User content: record scans, photos, collection entries, notes, and related metadata you upload or create.
  • Support data: information you provide in support requests, feedback, or legal/privacy enquiries.
  • Device and usage data: device model, OS version, app version, language, crash logs, diagnostics, IP-derived region, interaction data.
  • Transaction data: subscription product and billing status information received from app stores; payment card details are processed by Apple/Google and not stored by VinylScan.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: where applicable on the website, we and our providers may use cookies or similar tools for analytics, security, and functionality.

2. How We Use Information

We use personal data to:

  • provide and operate the VinylScan Service;
  • identify records and generate valuation-related outputs;
  • maintain accounts, subscriptions, and customer support;
  • improve features, reliability, and security;
  • detect misuse, fraud, abuse, or policy violations;
  • send service messages and, where permitted, product updates and marketing communications;
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our legal rights.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (UK/EU)

Where applicable, we process personal data under one or more of these legal bases:

  • performance of a contract with you;
  • our legitimate interests (for example, service improvement, security, and support);
  • compliance with legal obligations;
  • your consent, where required by law.

You may object to processing based on legitimate interests in certain circumstances.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data for money. We may share personal data with:

  • service providers that help us host, analyze, secure, and support the service;
  • app distribution and payment platforms (such as Apple App Store and Google Play) as required for subscriptions;
  • professional advisers, auditors, insurers, and legal representatives;
  • law enforcement or regulators when legally required;
  • a buyer or successor in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset sale.

5. International Transfers

Your personal data may be processed in countries outside your home jurisdiction, including the United States and other locations where our providers operate. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards for cross-border data transfers.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for service delivery, account administration, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and legitimate business purposes. Retention periods vary depending on the type of data and purpose of processing.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, and receive a portable copy of certain personal data. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise rights, contact us at privacy@vinylscanapp.com. We may ask for identity verification before processing requests.

8. Marketing and Notifications

We may send service-related notifications needed to operate your account. Where allowed by law, we may send marketing communications. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails using the unsubscribe link or by contacting us.

9. Security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including access controls, encryption in transit where appropriate, and operational safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

The VinylScan Service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

11. Third-Party Services

The VinylScan Service may link to third-party websites or services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, and we are not responsible for those practices.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When material changes are made, we will post the updated version with a revised date. Continued use of the VinylScan Service after updates are posted means you accept the revised policy.
